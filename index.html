Tuyu (ツユ)

@TUYU_official


The group was formed on June 12, 2019. Its name comes from the Japanese word for “rainy season” duringwhich it was formed.

×

☂ About

Tuyu is Currently has over 1,260,000 YouTube Subscribers. In addition to its showpiece titled “Compared Child” that has had over 43million views, every music video that they have uploaded have been played over a million times. They are constantly ranked in both Japanese and foreign music distribution sites and appear in their popular playlists. A group of artists to keep an eye on with many fans around the world.

☂ Member

A music unit consisting of Pusu on guitar and in charge of composing the songs, Rei as vocals, and miro on piano.

Member of Tuyu :

Pusu (Pusu(ぷす))

Composert, Guitarist

Rei (Rei(礼衣))

Vocals
